Frequently Asked Questions

Find Answers to Common Questions About Our Courses and Services

How do I enroll in a course?

To enroll in a course, simply browse our courses page, select the course you're interested in, and click on "Enroll Now". You'll need to create an account or login if you already have one, then proceed with the payment process. Once payment is confirmed, you'll get immediate access to the course materials.

Can I enroll in multiple courses at once?

Yes, you can enroll in multiple courses simultaneously. We offer bundle packages at discounted rates for students who want to take multiple courses. Simply add the desired courses to your cart and proceed with enrollment.

Are there any prerequisites for the courses?

Most of our beginner courses don't require any prior knowledge. However, advanced courses may have prerequisites. Each course page clearly mentions any required background knowledge or skills. If you're unsure, you can always contact our support team for guidance.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, RuPay), net banking, UPI, digital wallets (Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay), and EMI options for select courses. All transactions are secure and processed through trusted payment gateways.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Yes, we offer a 7-day refund policy. If you're not satisfied with the course within the first 7 days of enrollment, you can request a full refund. Please check our refund policy page for detailed terms and conditions. The refund process typically takes 5-7 business days.

How long do I have access to the course materials?

Once enrolled, you get lifetime access to the course materials including video lectures, study notes, and practice questions. You can learn at your own pace and revisit the materials anytime. We also provide regular updates to the course content at no additional cost.

Are the courses self-paced or instructor-led?

Most of our courses are self-paced, allowing you to learn according to your schedule. However, we also offer instructor-led live sessions for select premium courses. You can check the course details page to see the format and schedule.

Do you provide study materials?

Yes, we provide comprehensive study materials including video lectures, PDF notes, practice questions, and assignments. All materials are designed by expert faculty and are regularly updated to match the latest curriculum and exam patterns.

Do you provide certificates upon completion?

Yes, we provide certificates of completion for all our paid courses. The certificate is issued once you successfully complete all the modules and assessments. You can download and share your certificate on LinkedIn and other professional platforms. The certificate includes a unique verification code.

Are the certificates recognized?

Our certificates are recognized by various organizations and can be used to enhance your resume. While they may not have formal academic accreditation, they demonstrate your commitment to learning and skill development in the respective field.

What are the system requirements for accessing courses?

You need a computer or mobile device with internet connection. For the best experience, we recommend using the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, or Safari browsers. Videos can be streamed in HD quality, and study materials are available in PDF format.

Can I download the videos for offline viewing?

Currently, videos are available for streaming only through our platform. However, you can download all study materials, notes, and assignments for offline use. We're working on introducing offline video access for premium courses soon.

Is there a mobile app available?

Our website is fully mobile-responsive and works well on all devices. We're currently developing dedicated mobile apps for Android and iOS, which will be available soon. You'll be notified once the apps are released.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team through multiple channels: Email us at support@drisitclasses.com, call us at +91 98765 43210 (Mon-Sat, 9 AM - 6 PM), or use the contact form on our website. We typically respond within 24 hours.

Do you offer corporate training?

Yes, we offer customized corporate training programs for businesses and organizations. We can tailor courses according to your specific requirements and deliver them online or at your premises. Please contact our corporate training team for more details.

How often are the course contents updated?

We regularly update our course content to ensure it remains current and relevant. Technical courses are updated every 3-4 months, while fundamental courses are reviewed annually. You'll receive notifications about major updates to your enrolled courses.
