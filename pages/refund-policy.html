Refund Policy

Our Commitment to Your Satisfaction

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

At Dris IT Classes, we want you to be completely satisfied with your learning experience. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds and cancellations of our paid courses.

7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for most of our courses. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can request a full refund within 7 days of enrollment.

Satisfaction Guaranteed

Try any course risk-free for 7 days. If it's not right for you, get a full refund - no questions asked.

Refund Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for a refund, your request must meet the following conditions:

Time Frame

Request must be made within 7 days (168 hours) of course enrollment

Course Progress

You must not have completed more than 20% of the course content

Material Downloads

You must not have downloaded more than 3 study materials or videos

Certificate Status

You must not have received a course completion certificate

Non-Refundable Items

The following items are not eligible for refunds:

Free Courses and Materials

All free courses, notes, and study materials are non-refundable

Discounted Bundles

Courses purchased as part of special promotional bundles

Corporate Training

Custom corporate training programs and workshops

Services Rendered

Consulting services, personalized tutoring, and one-on-one sessions

Refund Process

1

Submit Request

Contact our support team via email at refunds@drisitclasses.com with your order details and reason for refund

2

Review Process

Our team will review your request within 2-3 business days and verify eligibility

3

Approval

If approved, we'll process your refund and send you a confirmation email

4

Refund Processing

Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method

Information Required for Refund Request

To process your refund request efficiently, please provide the following information:

Full name and registered email address
Order ID or transaction reference number
Course name and enrollment date
Brief reason for refund request
Contact phone number (optional)

Refund Methods

Refunds are processed to the original payment method used for purchase:

Credit/Debit Cards

Refunded to the original card within 5-7 business days

Net Banking

Refunded to the original bank account within 7-10 business days

UPI/Digital Wallets

Refunded to the original wallet within 3-5 business days

Partial Refunds

In certain circumstances, we may offer partial refunds:

Course Progress 20-50%

50% refund if you've completed between 20-50% of the course

Limited Downloads

75% refund if you've downloaded some materials but not extensively used the course

Technical Issues

Partial refund if technical issues prevent course access (case-by-case basis)

Course Transfers

If you're not satisfied with your current course, you may request a transfer to another course of equal or lesser value within the 7-day refund period.

Only one transfer per enrollment is allowed
Price difference will be charged or refunded accordingly
Transfer requests must meet standard refund eligibility criteria

Exceptions

We reserve the right to deny refund requests in the following situations:

Policy Abuse

Multiple refund requests for the same course or pattern of abuse

Violation of Terms

If you've violated our Terms and Conditions or engaged in prohibited activities

Fraudulent Activity

Suspicion of fraudulent enrollment or payment
