Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our courses and services

How do I enroll in a course?

To enroll in a course, simply browse our courses page, select the course you're interested in, and click on "Enroll Now". You'll need to create an account or login if you already have one, then proceed with the payment process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and digital wallets. All transactions are secure and processed through trusted payment gateways.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Yes, we offer a 7-day refund policy. If you're not satisfied with the course within the first 7 days of enrollment, you can request a full refund. Please check our refund policy for detailed terms and conditions.

How long do I have access to the course materials?

Once enrolled, you get lifetime access to the course materials including video lectures, study notes, and practice questions. You can learn at your own pace and revisit the materials anytime.

Do you provide certificates upon completion?

Yes, we provide certificates of completion for all our paid courses. The certificate is issued once you successfully complete all the modules and assessments. You can download and share your certificate.
