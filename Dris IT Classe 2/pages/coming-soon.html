Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our upcoming features

When will the new features be available?

We're working on a phased rollout. The Online Admission Portal will be available in Q1 2024, followed by the Test Series Platform in Q2 2024. Other features will be released throughout 2024.

Will there be additional costs for new features?

Most new features will be included in our existing course packages at no additional cost. Some premium features may have separate pricing, which will be clearly communicated before launch.

How can I join the beta testing program?

You can apply for our beta testing program by clicking the "Apply for Beta Access" button above. We'll review your application and contact you with next steps.

Will the mobile app be free?

Yes, the mobile app will be free to download for all registered students. You'll need an active subscription to access premium content.
